What is Kanal and Marla?

+ -

Kanal and Marla are most prominently used in Himachal Pradesh especially in Knagra , Hamirpur etc. Usually 1 Kanal is equal to the 20 20 Marlas. But it is still different parts of Himachal Pradesh.

What is Bigha and Biswa?

+ -

Bigha and Biswa both are the Land measurement unit. These are most commonly used in Shimla regions of Solan, Sirmaur. The Biswa and Bigha are measured in different way in the different regions of Himachal Pardesh. Thus, it is difficult to calculate the accurate result.

For Further information, visit himbhumi.nic.in

Some details of Karam?

+ -

The usage of Square Karam for the measurement of land is prevalent in the states of Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, and Punjab.However, it is a traditional units of measuring small lands, including farmlands, residential lands, and commerical lands in rural areas, usually below one acre. One Karam is equivalent to 5.5 feet .
